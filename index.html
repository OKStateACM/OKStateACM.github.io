OKState ACM

OSU's Computer Science Professional Skills Development Organization

Welcome to the ACM at OSU!

The Oklagoma State University chapter of the Association for Computing Machinery meets Wednesdays at 5:30, starting in the Computer Science lounge in MSCS 206. We aim to provide a well-rounded introduction to various aspects of the Computer Science professional field.

Upcoming Events

Over the course of the Spring 2017 semester, our weekly meetings will cover topics such as web design/development, marketing yourself to employers, generative art, and more! In addition to the weekly meetings, we will be hosting an artificial intelligence workshop in February, OSU's first-ever hackathon in March, and a cybersecurity/penetration testing workshop in April. For a complete and updated list of all of our events, check out our calendar!

Outreach

The OKState ACM is proud to announce partnership with Will Rogers Elementary School in creating the Level Up Coding weekly program, in which OSU students introduce fourth- and fifth-graders to the world of programming by teaching them how to write games in JavaScript.
